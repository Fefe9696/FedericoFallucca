I am one of the organizers of Geosem 2025-2026, a seminar series in Algebraic Geometry and related fields held at the University of Trento.
Between 2020 and 2023, I have been one of the organizers, along with Enrico Savi and Sara Sottile, of the Doc in Progress series at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Trento, which consists of a series of meetings with the purpose of dissemination. The speakers are selected among current PhD students, as the name suggests, in Mathematics or related fields.
