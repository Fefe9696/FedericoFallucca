I am actually the Teaching Assistant of Geometry and Linear Algebra and Analysis 2 courses. I am looking for a postdoc position in Algebraic Geometry. I would like to investigate new topics and open problems not only related to my current research but also enrlage my mathematical horizons.
In most of my research, I focus on the study of surfaces of general type. Specifically, I construct surfaces such as abelian coverings or product-quotient surfaces to obtain new relevant examples. Additionally, I seek to classify abelian coverings and product-quotient surfaces for small values of certain invariants, such as the Euler characteristic and the self-intersection of a canonical divisor. Recently, I have also been investigating various open questions related to the canonical map of surfaces of general type, particularly examining the canonical map of abelian coverings and product-quotient surfaces.
