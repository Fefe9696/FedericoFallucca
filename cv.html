Currently I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the University of Trento, under the supervision of Prof. Roberto Pignatelli. The expected date of the defense of my PhD thesis is September 2023.
In most of my research, I focus on the study of surfaces of general type. Specifically, I construct surfaces as abelian coverings or Product-Quotient surfaces in order to obtain new relevant examples. Additionally, I seek to classify abelian coverings and Product-Quotient surfaces for small values of certain invariants, such as the Euler characteristic and the selfintersection of a canonical divisor. Recently, I have also been investigating various open questions related to the canonical map of surfaces of general type, particularly examining the canonical map of abelian coverings and Product-Quotient surfaces.
